Hummus

Made from a handful of fresh ingredients, and it only takes minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 30 oz Chickpeas
  • 1/3 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Tahini
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 Lemons
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to your blender or high-powered blender and secure the lid. Remove the lid cap and insert the tamper.
  2. Turn the blender on high for 30 seconds (or more for a creamier texture) and use the tamper to push the hummus into the blades. Add more chickpea liquid, if desired, for a softer hummus.
  3. Add the hummus to a serving plate and garnish with olive oil, paprika and fresh parsley.
